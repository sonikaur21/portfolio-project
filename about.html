Sonia Kaur Web Studio is a web design and development studio focused on creating clean, modern, and user-friendly websites for small businesses and creatives.
Founded by Sonia Kaur, a front-end developer based in the USA, the studio is built on a strong foundation of cross-disciplinary thinking, communication, and design. Sonia’s background in language education, translation, and intercultural communication brings a thoughtful, user-centered approach to every project.
Now working deeply in front-end development, Sonia Kaur Web Studio builds responsive, dynamic websites using modern technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Each project is designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind, ensuring websites are clear, intuitive, and effective.
Beyond development, Sonia is also a community builder and creative — co-founder of ChattaLatin, a local initiative connecting people through Latin dance and culture. This experience informs the studio’s approach: building digital spaces that connect, communicate, and inspire.
